Scuba diving

At the eastern coast of India, Pondicherry Scuba diving is probably the best ride to adventure that one can get. Scuba diving in Pondicherry has gained a lot of popularity over the years as this place is the home to a lot of underwater experiences that one cannot find even in the west coast counterparts of this town.

Rock Beach

Rock beach as the name suggests has lot of big rocks lining up the shore so it's not really accessible along the complete strip. The area around rock beach is a happening area and pedestrian only which is a great initiative. It's highly recommended during sunset.

white Town

"White Town" is the French Quarters in Pondicherry primarily the French families still living there and the houses being converted into heritage hotels and restaurants and art galleries. A very well planned clean laid out area with trees providing a continuous shade and buildings dominated with pale yellow color.

Notre Dame Des Anges Church

Also known as Our Lady of Angels Church, it is the most famous church in Pondicherry. The original structure was built in 1855 by Napoleon the third and painted white from both inside and outside

Golden Globe

The Geodesic dome is covered by golden discs and reflects sunlight, which gives the structure its characteristic radiance. Inside the central dome is a meditation hall known as the inner chamber - this contains the largest optically-perfect glass globe in the world.
